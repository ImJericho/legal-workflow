Writ Petition under Article 226 of Constitution of India

For issuance of prerogative writ of mandamus or any other appropriate writ

Most Respectfully Showeth:

  1. That the petitioner is a citizen of India residing at ________.
    The respondent is Municipal Corporation of Delhi having its office at Town Hall, Chandni Chowk, Delhi.

BRIEF FACTS:-

  1. That the petitioner is aggrieved by the illegal appointments of daily wage workers by the M.C.D. office in defiance of Notification No. MCD/LF/01-103 dated 1.2.2014 which requires the M.C.D. to appoint only those people as daily wage worker who are below the age of 30 years as on 01.10.2014. The said Notification was issued after it was duly approved.

Grounds:

  1. Because the action of the respondent is contrary to law and good conscience.
  2. Because the action of the respondent is arbitrary, unreasonable, irrational and unconstitutional.
  3. Because the respondent have no right to play with the career of the petitioner.
  1. That the cause of action arose on 1.2.2014 when the respondent issued Notification No. MCD/LF/01-103 dated 1.2.2014; further arose on 1.12.2014 when the petitioner was removed despite eligibility; further arose on appointment of ineligible persons; further arose on representations made on 1.12.2014 and 2.1.2015; and further continues by inaction of the respondent.
  2. That the petitioner has no other efficacious remedy except to approach this Hon’ble Court by way of this writ petition.

Prayer:

  1. Issue an appropriate writ in the nature of mandamus or any other writ directing the respondents to cancel the illegal appointments made in disregard of Notification No. MCD/LF/01-103 dated 1.2.2014;
  2. Issue necessary directions for appointment of the petitioner;
